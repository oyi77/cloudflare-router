### Codebase Patterns
- asyncHandler wraps all async routes: (fn) => (req, res, next) => Promise.resolve(fn(...)).catch(next)
- APIError class: new APIError(message, statusCode, code)
- authMiddleware applied to all /api routes after /auth/login and /auth/check
- APP_PROCESSES = Map<name, ChildProcess> in server.js (in-memory process registry)
- CONFIG_DIR = path.join(process.env.HOME, '.cloudflare-router')
- APPS_YAML = path.join(CONFIG_DIR, 'apps.yaml')
- execSync used for nginx/tunnel commands; exec for background tasks
